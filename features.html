ABOUT WOLFCRAFT

Learn about the WolfCraft Servers, what features we offer our players and the plugins we use to enchance player experience.

WHO ARE WE?


The WolfCraft Community is gaming focused community centered around our Minecraft Servers. Being part of our community means joining a group of people who care and support each other.

FEATURES WE PROVIDE


CoreProtect

To protect player builds from unwanted damage and distruction, we have equiped our servers with the CoreProtect plugin. CoreProtect is a fast, efficient, data logging and anti-griefing tool allowing us to rollback and restore any amount of damage. To learn more about the features CoreProtects offers to ptoect your build, click here.

Player-Generated-Economy

At WolfCraft, we offer players a functional economy to buy and trade. Each season, players are given the opportunity to build uniquly styled shops and stalls to sell their items in our desginated Shopping District. To see past examples of shoping districts we have constructed, visit the gallery.

Dynamic World Map

To help players find the perfect spot for this build and/or visualise their builds from a different perspective, each season we have a live dynamic map of our server showing the locations of builds, players and their movements in real time. To view the current season's map, click here.

Discord Community

Joinig WolfCraft grants you access to our official Discord Server. This is a happening place where you can relax, connect with other members, ask questions or even share your work for other to see. You will be able to access gaming channels dedicated to other games outside Minecraft. Furthermore, this is where you can communicate with staff to help us improve your experience on the server.

International & Diverse Community

The WolfCraft community has over a hundred players thus far and is growing each WolfCraft season. We are proud to also have a international community of players from different backgrounds and countries providing a diverse and unique playing experience.
