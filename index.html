WELCOME TO WOLFCRAFT

A MINECRAFT GAMING COMMUNITY

WHO ARE WE?

We are a gaming community aimed at providing players with a casual, engaging and mature environment in unwind and have fun.

EXPLORE THE VAST WORLD OF WOLFCRAFT

Wolfcraft provides players with an opportunity to experience a variety of game-play styles - all in one place. Our community is centered around our three Minecraft servers - each providing you with an unique playing experience. Check out our features section for more information on our servers and community.

SURVIVAL

This is the server your after if your looking for a near-perfect vanilla experience while being assured that your builds and actions are protected. Additionally, you will find many game-play easing datapacks that help make some challenging tasks easier to accomplish. These datapacks are voted for by the community and are evaluated regularly to ensure our server meets our player's expectations.

CREATIVE

Want to explore the creative boundaries of minecraft with others? Then this is the server for you. Our creative server offers players with a vast expansive flat-world where you can freely collaberate with friends on projects, plan your future bases, or have a little fun.

MODDED

Need a place to get away from the vanilla experience or recharge your batteires after a long build? Our new modded server is now open for business. With this server we provide our players with new engaging game-play options that help expand the creative horizons of possibilities in minecraft. With new mod's voted for by the community every few months, you can be assured to always have something new to experience.
